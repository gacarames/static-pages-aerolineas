Change and refund conditions

Conditions:

Minimum of 3 (three) and maximum of 12 (twelve) flight coupons.
Once issued the purchase of additional coupons is not allowed (even if maximum of 12 coupons has not been reached).
Maximum stay of 90 days from the date of the first Visit Argentina flight.
Booking and issuing must be done with a minimal anticipation of 21 days prior to the date of the first flight.
All taxes and additional charges will be collected at time of issue.

Ticketing deadlines:

Bookings made with more than 30 (thirty) days in advance can be issued up to 30 (thirty) days before departure of the first flight.
Bookings made within 30 (thirty) to 22 (twenty two) days before departure of the first flight must be issued between 24 hours after the reservation has been made.

Refunds:

Before departure of first flight booked original amount will be refund, less a cancellation fee of USD50.
After departure of first flight booked: Refund is not allowed.
Penalty for no-show: Charge of USD75 will apply.
Date changes: USD50 per transaction, any time, within allowed maximum stay.
Change of routing: USD50 per transaction, any time, subject to availability of seats in the same fare basis.
In case fare is higher, difference will be charged, in case fare is lower, difference will not be refunded.

AR PLUS

VARG flights will accrue miles at promotional class level.
Luggage allowance and excess charges
Luggage allowance for Visit Argentina flights is 1 (one) piece of baggage of 23kgs (50,7 pounds).
Standard baggage rates will apply to the exceeding weight of this allowance.
