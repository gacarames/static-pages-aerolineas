Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral Reservation System and on-line payment

Before making a reservation or consulting our schedules and fares, please read carefully the general conditions of our On-line Reservation and payment System.

1. Inquiries

To ask about Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral's flight schedules, availability and prices, you don't need to enter your personal information or credit card number.

2. Bookings

You may book tickets in Aerolineas Argentinas and Austral flights up to a maximum of 9 (nine) tickets (for you and 6 passengers) each time (maximum: 3 times per day)..

Once you have made the online reservation, you will automatically get a reservation code (subject to a date of expiration). Within Germany you shall contact our local Aerolineas Argentinas office in Frankfurt by email in order to arrange the payment.

To ensure getting your tickets at the estimated price, they must be purchased in the country of origin.

3. Payment and Ticket Issue

Payment and Online Ticket Issuance

All passengers who pay for their tickets at aerolineas.com save their booking fee.
Without prejudice Aerolineas Argentinas informs that certain promotions or special dates may apply a management fee. In this paticular, when paid, the price will include the same.

You can pay your bookings made at our Argentina website with a credit cards American Express, Argencard, Cabal, Diners, MasterCard, Nativa Visa, Nativa MasterCard, Tarjeta Naranja or Visa and with Pago Fácil and Pago Mis Cuentas, and issue your electronic ticket within 1 hr of your booking. This service is available to all routes operated by Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral.

For any complaint or inquiry for the goods and/or services purchased by AMEX members, the payer must previously contact Aerolineas Argentinas at Call to 0810-222-VOLAR (0810-222-86527) - http://www.aerolineas.com.ar/en-ar/info-for-your-trip/contact-information.

Should your booking not be paid through our website, you may purchase your ticket at any of our offices.

Online payment from/to Lima is only available for bookings for flights scheduled at least 7 consecutive days from the booking date.

Refunds

If your fare allows a refund, it must be requested through the online form created for this purpose. In case of payment through Pago Fácil or Pago Mis Cuentas, the refund will be made in cash. You will receive a voucher number by e-mail; present it at our nearest Sales Office to get the refund. For amounts over ARS 10,000, we will contact you to arrange date and place, so we make sure that our office has the necessary cash.

This service is available to all routes operated by Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral.

Dangerous Items

We remind you of the objects you can't carry with yourself or in your luggage:

  • Scissors
  • Pliers
  • Knives
  • Cutter
  • Limes
  • Knitting needles
  • Punch
  • Screwdriver
  • Cutting or piercing items in general

And what elements you can't carry on board or in your checked baggage:

  • Explosives: Signal flares, Firecrackers, Spalklers, etc.

  • Flamables: Fuel, Paints, Solvents, Lighter fluid, matches, etc.

  • Weapons: Loaded Firearms, Ammunition, Gun Powder, Pepper Spray or Tear Gas (Unloaded firearms must only be accepted as checked baggage by airport police or proper authority), etc.

  • Corrosive: Battery leakage, Lavandin, ammonia, oven cleaners, multipurpose cleaners, etc.

  • Other dangerous materials: Gasoline-Powered tools, Camping equipment with fuel, radioactive materials (including radio pharmaceuticals), Oxidizers, Corrosives, Poisons, Infections substances, etc.

Security Conditions

We know that security in sending personal information is a major concern for customers who use the Internet. Although Aerolíneas Argentinas S.A and Austral Líneas Aéreas request your credit card information only when you make a reservation, we wish to inform you that we have worked together with Amadeus to ensure that information cannot be accessed by a third party.

Security offered in this medium is provided through SLL(Secure Socket Label) technology, which is based on encoding communications produced between your personal computer and our server, thus preventing their interception.

Do not worry about not receiving any notice telling you that you are entering a "secure" mode, since this is because the design of the site uses frames that enable screen division into areas that can be independently updated.

If you want to verify that this is indeed a secure site, while on the RESERVAS (Reservations) page you can right click on the "Consultar" (Ask) image or "Ingresar" (Enter) and select "Abrir vínculo en una ventana nueva" (Open Link on a New Window). This will create a new window where only the reservation pages will be loaded and where you can see the lock and the correct URL with the prefix "https" on the upper address (secure mode).
