PASSENGERS WITH DISABILITIES

Accommodating the special needs of passengers with disabilities is a top priority for Aerolineas Argentinas. We offer a variety of special services to such passengers (please put a mark in the square alongside of each described service you may need) including:

  • Pre-reserved seats. The adjacent seats under certain circumstances when customer with disabilities must travel with a companion for assistance.
  • Accommodate carry-on assistive devices like canes, crutches, and walkers small enough to be located in the cabin and the overhead compartment.
  • Approved and authorized electronic medical assistive devices, such as respirators, ventilators, etc., that uses non-spillable batteries maybe accepted in the cabin on an individual basis provided they meet certain safety testing standards.
  • Wheelchairs. Motorized and non-motorized assisted devices for transport.
  • Boarding Assistance
  • Medical Oxygen. Customers requiring in-flight medical oxygen may use only FAA-approved portable oxygen concentrators during all phases of the flight. A forty-eight hour advance notice is required to complete medical paperwork. Please contact reservations at 1-800-333-0276 to arrange for assistance.
  • Transportation to, from, and between gates either by wheelchair or in several locations electric carts.
  • Enplaning and Deplaning Assistance.
  • Assistance with visual, auditory, cognitive, or mobility impairments while in the airport and on the aircraft.
  • Dietary needs, with proper notice by calling 1-800-333-0276
  • Consideration of your needs during extended tarmac delays.

Please specify any other needs that you may have while traveling and Aerolineas Argentinas will do its best to accommodate those needs you may have.
We have employees at the airports who are trained as compliant resolution officials (CROs) and are available during operating hours. You may request a CRO by containing an Aerolineas Argentinas agent at the airport.
Consumers flying to or from the United States can obtain copies of the regulations regarding non-discrimination on the basis of disability in air travel in an accessible format from the United States Department of Transportation by the following means:

  • Toll-free Hotline for air travelers with disabilities at 1-800-778-4838 or 1-800-455-9880.
  • By telephone to the Aviation Consumer Protection Division at 202-366-2220 (voice) or (202) 366-0511 (TTY).
  • By mail to the Air Consumer Protection Division, C-75, U.S. Department of Transportation, 1200 New Jersey Avenue, SE, West Building, Room W-96-432, Washington, DC 2059 and on the Aviation Consumer Protection Division’s website (http:airconsumer.ost.dot.gov). Again if you need disability services, please place a mark alongside of the described services above.

Dangerous Items

We remind you of the objects you can't carry with yourself or in your luggage:

  • Scissors
  • Pliers
  • Knives
  • Cutter
  • Limes
  • Knitting needles
  • Punch
  • Screwdriver
  • Cutting or piercing items in general

And what elements you can't carry on board or in your checked baggage:

  • Explosives: Signal flares, Firecrackers, Spalklers, etc.

  • Flamables: Fuel, Paints, Solvents, Lighter fluid, matches, etc.

  • Weapons: Loaded Firearms, Ammunition, Gun Powder, Pepper Spray or Tear Gas (Unloaded firearms must only be accepted as checked baggage by airport police or proper authority), etc.

  • Corrosive: Battery leakage, Lavandin, ammonia, oven cleaners, multipurpose cleaners, etc.

  • Other dangerous materials: Gasoline-Powered tools, Camping equipment with fuel, radioactive materials (including radio pharmaceuticals), Oxidizers, Corrosives, Poisons, Infections substances, etc.

Security Conditions

We know that security in sending personal information is a major concern for customers who use the Internet. Although Aerolíneas Argentinas S.A and Austral Líneas Aéreas request your credit card information only when you make a reservation, we wish to inform you that we have worked together with Amadeus to ensure that information cannot be accessed by a third party.

Security offered in this medium is provided through SLL(Secure Socket Label) technology, which is based on encoding communications produced between your personal computer and our server, thus preventing their interception.

Do not worry about not receiving any notice telling you that you are entering a "secure" mode, since this is because the design of the site uses frames that enable screen division into areas that can be independently updated.

If you want to verify that this is indeed a secure site, while on the RESERVAS (Reservations) page you can right click on the "Consultar" (Ask) image or "Ingresar" (Enter) and select "Abrir vínculo en una ventana nueva" (Open Link on a New Window). This will create a new window where only the reservation pages will be loaded and where you can see the lock and the correct URL with the prefix "https" on the upper address (secure mode).
