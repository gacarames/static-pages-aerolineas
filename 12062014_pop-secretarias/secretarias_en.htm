Program Details .
Aerolíneas Argentinas Club Secretarias is a program of accumulation of points which grants benefits and permits to win interesting prizes quarterly. This Program has been designed exclusively for Secretaries with the purpose of gratifying the fidelity day by day deposit in our Company at the moment of managing a trip of the executives of the company.
HOW TO PARTICIPATE?
In order to participate in the Club Secretarias Program, you will have to fill the data of the request attached and follow the described indications in it. Do not forget to consign in the 'Chief-Executive Registered' box the complete names of the executives to whom you assist (no more than three), which will have to be registered in the Aerolíneas Plus Program.
If your executive has not become a member of the Aerolíneas Plus Program yet you may register him/her entering:
HOW TO OBTAIN POINTS?
Points are accumulated through the trips that your executive or functionaries make according to the following table. The more they travel by Aerolíneas Argentinas, more points shall be added up to your account.
TABLE OF POINTS FOR TRIPS MADE (VALUE PER SEGMENT)

HOW TO WIN PRIZES?
To be one of the winners of the Club Secretarias Program You will have to add up more than 40 points in your account during the corresponding quarter.

The points are added up automatically when your Chief or Executive present the Aerolíneas Plus Card at the moment of boarding or when you inform your Aerolíneas Plus membership number at the moment of making the booking.

WHICH ARE THE PRIZES?
First Prize: 2 (two) tickets to Regional destinations
Second Prize: 2 (two) tickets to National destinations
In some quarters you will probably have more prizes.
