Articles not allowed

We remind you of the objects you can't carry with yourself or in your luggage:

  • Scissors
  • Pliers
  • Knives
  • Cutter
  • Limes
  • Knitting needles
  • Punch
  • Screwdriver
  • Cutting or piercing items in general

And what elements you can't carry on board or in your checked baggage:

  • Explosives:

    Signal flares, Firecrackers, Spalklers, etc.

  • Flamables:

    Fuel, Paints, Solvents, Lighter fluid, matches, etc

  • Compressed Gases:

    Spray cans, Butane fuel, Scuba tanks, Oxigen bottles, etc.

  • Weapons:

    Loaded Firearms, Ammunition, Gun Powder, Pepper Spray or Tear Gas (Unloaded firearms must only be accepted as checked baggage by the airport police or proper authority) etc.

  • Corrosive:

    Battery leakage, Lavandin, ammonia, oven cleaners, multiporpuse cleaners, etc.

  • Another dangerous items:

    Gasoline-Powered tools, Camping equipment with fuel, radioactive materials (incluiding radio pharmaceuticals), Oxidizers, Corrosives, Poisons, Infections substances, etc.
