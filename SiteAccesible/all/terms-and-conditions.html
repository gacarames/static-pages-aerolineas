Step 1 - Flights

This step begins with the ticket booking process. Here the passenger begins their search by choosing the origin, destination, dates, class/cabin, and amount of passengers discriminated by age (Infant: up to 1 day before their 2nd birth date / children: from 2 to 1 day before their 12th birth date / adults: from 12 years of age).

According to the origin and destination of flights following options may be offered:

• A double entry table will be displayed on the right showing flights that operate on the selected date and on the row above the different fares and the applicable conditions and restrictions. The system will display the first flight with the cheapest rate for the chosen date. Different tabs with alternative dates (3 days before and 3 days after) will also be displayed on the top and include the lowest rate for each of them. If the request was for a return (there and back) this table will be displayed in the same way for the return leg.

• A list will be displayed with different flight options for one way with the lowest fare for each selection. If the display shows multiple results. At the bottom of the results there are pages that can be browsed. First options preselected will appears which you can be modified according to your convenience. Once the selection is made, you must click on 'Continue'. In the case of choosing "return dated", this process is repeated with the option “return“.

Step 2 - Check - Check your choice

This step will allow you to review the preliminary information of the selection made, to confirm that it has been carried out according to your wishes.

Here you will first see the final rate by the total number of selected passengers. On the next line, the breakdown by type of passenger by identifying price and taxes. It does not include the Administration Fee. This information is shown later in a yellow box.

It will also identify individually, the detail of each of the flights selected including origin and destination, dates, schedules, other airlines, possible stops, flight number, cabin selection (Business or economy) and type of aircraft.

In case of finding errors, passenger must start the booking process from scratch through the "New search" button at the top of the page.

Having accepted the selection, you can add your Aerolineas Plus account number so that data is automatically added or press "Continue without registering". In the case that you have a membership number and the number is not accepted, you must go to "My Plus account” log on for the first time” and generate a new request, after 48 hours, it will be enabled to be used again.

Step 3 - Data

In this step personal data for each of the passengers is required.. The fields marked with a red asterisk are compulsory obligatory.

Here you may also request special services (assistance for passengers deaf, blind or wheelchair need) and special dietary or religious meals.

Following you need to enter contact details including at least one phone number with area code and an email address.

Step 4 - Purchase

This step is a summary of the information of what has been carried out so far.
Total price (excluding applicable administration fees for sales in other stations).
Details of each of the flights.
Passenger information.
Information for payment (credit card).
Details of conditions of fares.
Additional information for the reservation, taxes that are not including, charges for certain destinations/airports, deadline for payment to finalize the reservation.
To continue with the confirmation of the reservation, the passenger must click on the box which establishes that they have read and accepted the conditions of the fare and hand luggage.
Finally you must click 'Confirm' to get the reservation code, which is the only information that confirms that the booking has been successfully finalized.

Step 5 - Confirmation

The reservation is displayed here. An email will, automatically be generated to the advised e-mail box, with the same information shown here. This information will remain in the system for up to 48 hours after the date and time that the last leg of the journey has been taken.
Then it erased from the system and goes into history and is stored in a permanent database, exclusive use of Aerolíneas Argentinas S.A.
The information includes final fare, the booking code and the breakdown of the confirmed flights and passenger data.
At the bottom of the screen allocation of seats is available for those fares that allow it and the printing of the reservation.
For tickets purchased through our US Website, you may cancel your reservation within 24 hours from the time of original booking for a full refund, so long as your reservation was made seven (7) days or more prior to your scheduled departure time. Timely cancellations may be made on Aerolineas Argentinas’ webpage (see "Cancellation form" under menu "Booking and Service") or by calling 1-800-333-0276. Please note that if you cancel a portion of a roundtrip itinerary of which the price was conditioned upon the purchase of a roundtrip ticket, you will need to cancel the entire itinerary and rebook your ticket.

Likewise, regarding some services we have reached accords with other airlines that will operate the plane you will travel with. Flights operated in this manner will be ruled by the Transport Conditions of such air companies. You can find details of these Transport Conditions in the website page of the company that operates such flights, or else, obtain it directly from the flight operator company.
