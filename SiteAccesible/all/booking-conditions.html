Conditions

Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral Reservation System.

Before making a reservation or consulting our schedules and fares, please read carefully the general conditions of our On-line Reservation System.

1. Inquiries

  • To ask about Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral's flight schedules, availability and prices, you don't need to enter your personal information or credit card number.

2. Bookings

  • You may book tickets in Aerolineas Argentinas and Austral flights up to a maximum of 9 (nine) tickets (for you and 8 passengers) each time (maximum: 3 times per day).
  • Once you have made the online reservation, you will automatically get a reservation code (subject to a date of expiration). Within Germany you shall contact our local Aerolineas Argentinas office in Frankfurt by email in order to arrange the payment.
  • To ensure getting your tickets at the estimated price, they must be purchased in the country of origin.

3. Payment and Issuance

Payment and Online Ticket Issuance

  • All passengers who pay for their tickets at aerolineas.com save their booking fee.
  • You can pay your bookings made at our Argentina website with a credit card Visa or MasterCard, and issue your electronic ticket. This service is available to all routes operated by Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral.
  • Online payment from/to Lima is only available for bookings for flights scheduled at least 7 consecutive days from the booking date.
  • Refunds
    Upon an online purchased ticket refund request, the same shall be made in person; the credit card must be produced, whereby the payer must sign the credit card receipt and the refund form. Refunds must be made within business hours of our closest sales offices.

Applicable charges (FEE) for changes

  • Check here the applicable charges (FEE) for changes made within the Argentinean territory on domestic, regional or international routes regardless of the place of issuance of tickets. They apply to both residents of Argentina and foreigners.

Electronic Ticket

  • The electronic ticket is a virtual ticket offered by Aerolíneas Argentinas through any of our sales channels, thus replacing the traditional ticket, with no additional cost. This document is more secure and allows you to save time, since you can show up at the airport just with your reservation code and a valid ID card.
  • This service is enabled for all routes operated by Aerolíneas Argentinas and Austral, except for London.
    Note: Dear customers, we inform you that Aerolineas Argentinas and Austral offer sale of tickets online in aerolineas.com site only. Therefore, they are not liable for sales offered through other sites.
  • Effective 12/19/2012 authentication service is active online for Visa card (Verify by Visa) and MasterCard (MasterCard SecureCode). It gives you the possibility to make purchases over the Internet quickly, easily, and with the same security that exists in face-to-face purchases.Please note that your card issuing bank may not be attached to this program.

Cancelling within 24 Hours of Booking.

  • You may cancel your reservation within 24 hours from the time of original booking for a full refund, so long as your reservation was made seven (7) days or more prior to your scheduled departure time. Timely cancellations may be made on Aerolineas Argentinas webpage (see Cancellation form under menu Booking and Service) or by calling 1-800-333-0276. Please note that if you cancel a portion of a roundtrip itinerary of which the price was conditioned upon the purchase of a roundtrip ticket, you will need to cancel the entire itinerary and rebook your ticket.

Unaccompanied Minors

To make a reservation for minors traveling alone you must contact our Call Center.

Domestic Flights / Regional / International Flights

  • Children under 2-6 years of age may not travel alone. If they are not accompanied by a relative or an adult, you should request a company-designated companion.
  • Children under 6-11 years of age may travel alone or you should request a company-designated companion.

International Passengers, Notice of Baggage Liability Limitations

Liability for loss, delay, or damage to baggage is limited unless a higher value is declared in advance and additional charges are paid. For most international travel (including domestic portions of international journeys) the liability limit is approximately US$ 9.07 per pound (US$ 20.00 per kilo) for checked baggage and US$ 400.00 per passenger for unchecked baggage. For travel wholly between US points, Federal rules require any limit on an airline baggage liability to be at least US$ 1,250.00 per passenger. Excess valuation may not be declared on certain types of articles. Some carriers assume no liability for fragile, valuable or perishable articles. Further information may be obtained from the carrier.
