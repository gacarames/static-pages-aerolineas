PIECE CONCEPT
When using the piece concept the luggage exceeds the allowed pieces or size and/or the weight. In each case the charges are as follow:
Quantity
Per each additional piece Basic fare
Volume
Per each piece between 158 cm and 203 cm Basic fare
Per each piece that exceeds 203 cm Double Fare (twice the Basic Fare)
Weight
Por cada pieza que exceda los 23/32 kg, . (según destino y/o clase de servicio) Basic fare
Excesos Combinados
In the case of combined excess (quantity, volume, weight)), the charges are cumulative.
